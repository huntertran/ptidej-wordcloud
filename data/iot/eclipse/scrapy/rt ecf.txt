Eclipse ECF provides frameworks for messaging and inter-process communications. We fully implement the OSGi Remote Services (RS) and Remote Service Admin (RSA) standards.
See the ECF Wiki for examples, tutorials, other documentation, as well as plans and efforts currently underway for future releases.
